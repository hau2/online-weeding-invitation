       Target timestamp calculation:
       - Combine weddingDate + weddingTime into Vietnam timezone target:
         `const targetStr = \`${weddingDate}T${weddingTime || '10:00'}:00+07:00\``
         `const targetMs = new Date(targetStr).getTime()`

       - useState for isPast (boolean)
       - useEffect with setInterval(1000ms):

         - Otherwise compute days/hours/minutes/seconds from diff
       - Cleanup: clearInterval on unmount

       Styling per template:
       - Traditional: dark card with gold text
       - Modern: dark gray card with white text
       - Minimalist: light gray card with dark text

       Mobile-first: cards sized responsively (w-12 h-14 on mobile, w-16 h-20 on desktop)
